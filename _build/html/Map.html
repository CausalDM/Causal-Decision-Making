Map#

In this section, we provide a structured map for the zoo of off-policy evaluation and optimization methods. The main purpose is to guide practitioners to find appropriate solutions for their problems, and hence to reduces the gap from academic research to real-world applications. We focus on several key decision points, including action, reward, features, policy classes, confounders, side information, etc.
